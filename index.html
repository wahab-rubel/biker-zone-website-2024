Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic
signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your
bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more
focused and legally compliant bike rider.

Check your helmet for visible signs of damage or wear.
If its been in an accident or is more than five years old, consider replacing it.

Inspect your bike for any signs of rust or wear,
check the tire pressure, brakes, and gears before riding.

Always ride in the same direction as traffic, use hand
signals for turns, and be aware of local laws regarding helmet use and bike paths.
